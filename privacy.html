1) Plain-English Overview

Forma helps you log Catholic habits, connect with friends for encouragement, join groups, and discover your parish community. We collect the data needed to run the Service and do not sell your personal information.

Forma does not track confession, state of grace, moral scoring, or personal intentions.

2) What We Collect and Store

A) Account and Authentication

  • Email address (for login and email verification)
  • User ID and authentication identifiers
  • Email verification status
  • Authentication method (email/password, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In)

When you use Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive basic profile information (name, email) from those providers. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider; we do not store plaintext passwords.

B) Profile Data

  • Display name
  • Timezone
  • Avatar selection (holy symbol icon)
  • Frame style and accent color preferences
  • Experience title, level, points, join date (generated/derived by the Service)
  • Nonprofit preference (which nonprofit you support)
  • Optional parish affiliation (parish name, city, state)

C) Activity and Social Data

  • Activity logs from a fixed catalog (e.g., Rosary, Mass, Adoration, Bible Study Group)
  • Timestamps (timezone-aware)
  • Optional short comment (intended to be brief and non-sensitive; limited length)
  • Friends data: friend code/QR, requests, accept/remove
  • Feed events and emoji reactions
  • Badges earned (earned permanently)
  • Computed stats (e.g., weekly goal progress and streaks)

D) Groups Data

  • Groups you create or join (group name, description, category)
  • Group posts and announcements you make
  • Reactions to group posts
  • Group membership status (member, owner)

E) Parishes Data

  • Parish affiliation (optional)
  • Parishes you search for or view
  • Parishes tagged to groups you create or join

F) Payment and Subscription Data

  • Subscription status (active, canceled, expired, trial)
  • Subscription plan type (individual monthly, individual yearly, family monthly, family yearly, family plus)
  • Payment transaction identifiers from App Store or Google Play
  • Billing cycle dates
  • Family plan membership (if applicable)

Payments are processed through the Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). We do not have access to your full payment card details. See Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies for details on their data handling.

G) Family Plan Data (If Using a Family Subscription)

  • Family membership status (owner or member)
  • Family group identifier
  • Which family plan you belong to

Family membership does not grant other family members access to your personal activity data or profile details beyond what is already visible to friends.

H) Technical and Security Data

  • Basic logs needed for security and reliability (e.g., IP address, request timestamps, error logs)
  • Used for abuse prevention, debugging, and protecting the Service

3) What Others Can See (and Where)

A) Global Friend Suggestions (In-App Discovery)

To logged-in users, Forma may show limited information for suggested users:

  • Display name
  • Experience title
  • Level
  • Points
  • Badge count
  • Join date

Not shown in global suggestions: nonprofit preference and parish fields.

By default, you are discoverable in friend suggestions. You can disable this by turning on "Hide from suggestions" in Settings.

B) Friends-Only Sharing

Your friends may see:

  • Your display name
  • Your feed events (activity type + time + optional short comment)
  • Reactions
  • Badges and related progress displays
  • Nonprofit preference on friend card ("Currently supporting {Nonprofit}")

C) Parish Discovery (Privacy-Focused)

Parish information is handled with care for your privacy:

  • Your parish affiliation is visible only to friends (not publicly discoverable)
  • The Parish Directory shows parishes and their associated groups, but does NOT list individual users who attend that parish
  • You CANNOT be added as a friend directly from a parish screen
  • You CAN be added as a friend from a group that is associated with a parish, if you are a member of that group
  • You may opt in or out of appearing in friend suggestions at any time

This design ensures that simply attending a parish does not expose your profile to strangers. Connection requires mutual participation in a group or direct sharing of your friend code.

D) Groups Visibility

Groups are public and discoverable by all users:

  • Group name, description, and category are visible to all users
  • Group member count is visible
  • Individual member list is visible to group members
  • Group posts are visible to group members
  • Groups may be tagged to parishes (visible to all users)

4) What You Should Not Share

Please do not enter sensitive personal data anywhere in the App (including comments), such as:

  • Confession details, sins, intentions, or sacramental state
  • Medical/mental health information
  • Addresses, phone numbers, financial information
  • Children's information
  • Precise location details

5) How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Operate accounts and authentication
  • Provide features (logging, feed, friends, reactions, badges)
  • Compute timezone-aware streaks and goals
  • Provide user-visible trend views (your own stats)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Prevent abuse and maintain reliability

6) Analytics / Tracking

We do not use third-party advertising trackers. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs unless disclosed here.

7) How We Share Data

We share data:

  • With your friends as described above (when you use social features)
  • With group members (when you participate in groups)
  • With service providers needed to operate the Service (e.g., authentication, hosting, database, payment processing via App Store and Google Play)
  • For legal and safety reasons when required by law or to protect users and the Service

We do NOT sell your personal information to anyone for any reason.

We do NOT accept advertising or paid promotions within the app.

Forma is independently operated with no outside investors. Your data is not shared with investors because there are none.

8) Retention and Data Export

We retain your account/profile/activity/social data until you delete your account or request deletion. This allows you to view your history over long periods if you keep using the Service.

Data export: You can export all your data at any time through the app (Settings > Export My Data). This provides a complete copy of your profile, activities, friends, groups, badges, and other data in a portable format.

We also maintain backups for reliability. When you delete your account:

  • We delete data from active systems within 14 days, and
  • Backup copies are retained for up to 30 days before being permanently deleted through normal backup rotation.

This 30-day backup retention period allows us to recover from accidental deletions or system failures while ensuring your data is fully removed within a reasonable timeframe.

9) Account Deletion and Your Rights

Deletion methods:

  • In-app delete (Settings) when available, and/or
  • Email support@virtueforma.faith from your verified account email

We aim to complete deletion from active systems within 14 days. Some data may persist in backups until overwritten through normal rotation.

You may request access or correction of your account data by contacting support.

10) Security

We use reasonable safeguards (e.g., encryption in transit via HTTPS and managed infrastructure access controls). No system is perfectly secure; use a strong password and avoid reusing passwords.

11) Children's Privacy

Forma is not intended for children under 13. If we learn we collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will update the "Last updated" date and version. Material changes may be communicated in-app or by email.
