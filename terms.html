8) Visibility, Sharing, and Discovery Features

Forma includes friends, a feed, reactions, badges, groups, parishes, and "friend suggestions."

A) Global Friend Suggestions (In-App Discovery)

When you are logged in, Forma may suggest other users to help you connect. Suggested users may show limited information such as:

  • Display name
  • Experience title (e.g., Seeker, Pilgrim)
  • Level
  • Points
  • Badge count
  • Join date

This is in-app discoverability (not a public website directory).

Not shown in global suggestions: nonprofit preference and parish affiliation.

B) Friends-Only Sharing (Feed and Friend Card)

When you are friends with someone, the Service may show:

  • Your display name
  • Your activity events in the feed (catalog activity + timestamp + optional short comment)
  • Emoji reactions
  • Badges and related progress displays
  • Nonprofit preference ("Currently supporting {Nonprofit}") on a friend card
  • Parish affiliation (if you have provided it)

C) Groups

Groups are covenant-centered communities within Forma:

  • Groups can be open (anyone can join) or closed (invite-only)
  • Group owners define a shared covenant (practice commitment)
  • Groups may be associated with one or more parishes
  • Group membership is visible to other group members
  • You can have up to 5 active groups at a time; completed groups don't count

D) Parish Discovery (Privacy-Focused)

The Parish Directory allows you to find parishes and groups associated with them. For your privacy:

  • The Parish Directory does NOT list individual users who attend a parish
  • You CANNOT be added as a friend directly from a parish screen
  • You CAN be added as a friend from a group associated with a parish, if you are a member of that group
  • Your parish affiliation is visible only to your friends, not publicly

This ensures that attending a parish does not automatically expose you to strangers. Connections require mutual group participation or direct sharing of your friend code.

E) Discoverability and Hide-from-Suggestions Toggle

By default, your profile is discoverable in friend suggestions. Forma includes a setting to hide your profile from global friend suggestions. If enabled, you will not appear in suggested users lists, but you may still be connectable by friend code/QR (and other explicit connection mechanisms).

12) Charitable Commitment (Nonprofit Donations)

A) How It Works

Forma commits to donating 10% of subscription revenue to charitable nonprofits selected by users.

Each user may select a preferred nonprofit from the available options in the app. Your selection determines where your portion of the charitable commitment is directed.

B) Disbursement Threshold

Donations to a given nonprofit are disbursed only after the accumulated amount for that nonprofit exceeds $50 by month-end. Amounts below this threshold are held in a temporary account controlled by the Operator until the threshold is reached.

C) Holding Period

If a nonprofit's accumulated donations do not reach the $50 threshold in a given month, the funds remain held and carry over to subsequent months until the threshold is met.

Your subscription payment is a payment for services, not a charitable donation. The charitable commitment is made by the Operator, not by you. You should not claim your subscription as a tax-deductible charitable contribution.
