B) Friends-Only Sharing

When you are friends with someone, the Service may show your display name, activity events in the feed, emoji reactions, badges, nonprofit preference, and parish affiliation.

C) Groups

Groups are public communities within Forma. Anyone can browse and join public groups. Group owners can post announcements; members can post and react. Groups may be associated with parishes. Group membership is visible to other group members.

D) Parish Discovery (Privacy-Focused)

The Parish Directory allows you to find parishes and groups associated with them. The Parish Directory does NOT list individual users who attend a parish. You CANNOT be added as a friend directly from a parish screen. Your parish affiliation is visible only to your friends, not publicly.

By default, your profile is discoverable in friend suggestions. Forma includes a setting to hide your profile from global friend suggestions.
