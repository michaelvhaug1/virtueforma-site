Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your Forma account and data

This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please export your data first if you want to keep a copy.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Forma account using either of these methods:

  • In-App Deletion (Recommended)
    Open Forma > Go to Settings > Scroll to "Delete Account" > Confirm deletion
  • Email Request
    Send an email to support@virtueforma.faith from the email address associated with your account. Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

Data Type Action
Account & Profile Deleted (email, display name, avatar, preferences)
Activity History Deleted (all logged activities, timestamps, comments)
Friends & Social Deleted (friend connections, feed posts, reactions)
Groups Membership removed; posts you made are deleted
Badges & Progress Deleted (all badges, streaks, points, levels)
Subscription Canceled (no further charges; manage via App Store/Google Play)

Retention Period

After you request deletion:

  • Active systems: Data is removed within 14 days
  • Backups: Retained for up to 30 days, then permanently deleted through normal backup rotation

This retention period allows us to recover from accidental deletions while ensuring your data is fully removed within a reasonable timeframe.

Before You Delete

Consider these options before deleting your account:

  • Export your data: Go to Settings > Export My Data to download a copy of all your information
  • Cancel subscription only: If you just want to stop paying, you can cancel your subscription through the App Store or Google Play without deleting your account
  • Take a break: Your account will remain intact if you simply stop using the app

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at support@virtueforma.faith.
