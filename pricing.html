Billing & Invoicing Terms

Manual Invoicing

All Family and Organization plans are billed manually. After you create your account, you will receive an invoice via email within 1-2 business days.

First Invoice Due Within 30 Days

Your first invoice is due within 30 days of account creation. You may begin using Forma immediately while your invoice is being processed.

Account Suspension for Non-Payment

VirtueForma reserves the right to suspend or cancel accounts if payment is not received within 30 days of the invoice due date. We will send reminders before any account action is taken.

Payment Methods

We accept payment via credit card, debit card, ACH bank transfer, or check. Payment instructions will be included in your invoice.

Important: Your organization will be created in "Pending Activation" status. Full access is granted immediately, but your account may be suspended if payment is not received within 30 days of your invoice date.

Free for Priests and Religious

We believe those who serve the Church should have free access to tools that support their ministry. Contact us for a clergy access code.
