Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Book a free intro session to see if Forma is right for you. If it's a fit, we'll set up your organization and provide invite codes for your members.

Can members still use the public Forma features?

Organization members see only their organization's private feed by default. They can still access public groups and parishes if desired.

How is billing handled?

We invoice monthly or annually. Parishes and dioceses can pay via check, ACH, or card. Schools can use purchase orders.

Is there a discount for dioceses or multi-parish deployments?

Yes! Contact us for custom pricing if you're deploying across multiple parishes or a whole diocese.

What about priests and religious in our organization?

Priests and religious can use their clergy access code for individual free access, or be included in your organization at no additional cost.
