Reset Activity History

Delete your activity data without deleting your account

Start fresh while keeping your account. This feature lets you clear your activity history while preserving your profile, friends, and badges.

How to Reset Your Activity History

Follow these steps in the Forma app:

  • Open Settings
    Tap the Settings icon in the app
  • Find "Reset Activity History"
    Scroll down to the Account section and tap "Reset Activity History"
  • Review what will be deleted
    Read the confirmation dialog carefully
  • Type "RESET" to confirm
    As a safety measure, you must type "RESET" to confirm the action

What Gets Deleted vs. Kept

Data Type Action
All logged activities Deleted
Current streak Deleted
Activity reactions Deleted
Account & profile Kept
Friends list Kept
Badges earned Kept
Group memberships Kept
Subscription status Kept

This action cannot be undone. Once you reset your activity history, all your logged activities and streaks are permanently deleted. Consider exporting your data first if you want to keep a record.

Why Reset Activity History?

Common reasons users choose to reset their activity:

  • Fresh start: Begin tracking your habits from scratch
  • Privacy: Clear your activity history while keeping your social connections
  • Testing: You were trying out the app and want to start for real

Need to Delete Everything?

If you want to delete your entire account including your profile, friends, and all data, visit Delete Your Account instead.

Questions?

If you have questions about resetting your activity or your data, contact us at support@virtueforma.faith.
